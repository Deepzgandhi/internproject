    Budgies are small colorful birds with playful,social and affectionate personalities.They are intelligent,curious and active.It forms stronng bond with thier owners.

    Height: 18-20 cm (7-8 inch) in length.
    Weight: 25-35 grams.
    Colors : Blue,green,yellow,grey and white.
    Lifespan :7-10 years

    Price : 300 to 700

  • Cockatiel

    Cockatiels are gentle birds,whith playful affectionate and social personalities.They are easy to train,whistle, and mimic human speech with a reputation for being great talkers.

    Height : 29-30 cm (11-13 inch).
    Weight: 100-120 grams
    Colors : Gray,white and yellow plumage.
    Lifespan : 15-20 years

    Price : 1,500 to 3,000

  • African Love Birds

    Love birds are short,colourful parrots known for thier playful,social and affectionate personalities.They thrive in pairs or groups,forming strong bonds.

    Height : 13-18 cms (5-7 inch)
    Weight: 40-60 grams
    Colors : Bright colors like blue,green,yellow,orange and red.
    Lifespan : 15-20 years

    Price : 2000 to 8,000

  • Finches

    Finches are small,social birds known for thier lively,energitic personalities and beautiful singing voices.They are easy to care for.And entertaining to watch,thriving in flocks..

    Height : 10-15 cm (4-6 inchs)
    Weight: 10-20 grams.
    Colors : Brown,gray,yellow,red and black.
    Lifespan : 5-10 years

    Price : 200 to 500

  • Conures

    Conures are medium sized playful birds known for thier bright colors,curved beaks,and mischievious personalities.It can be Loud and vocal with a high-pitched screech.

    Height : 28-38 cm (11-15 inch)
    Weight: 100-200 grams
    Colors :Green,blue,yellow,orange and red.
    Lifespan : 20-30 years

    Price : 2,000 to 10,000

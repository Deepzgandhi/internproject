CATS

  • Persian

    Persian cats are very friendly and prefer to be treated with gentleness. They love to be showered with affection and enjoy being petted for long periods of time.

    Height: For Males: 25-38 cm (10-15 in) , For Females : 23-36 cm (9-14 in).
    Weight: For Males: 4-7 kg (9-15 lb) For Females : 3-5 kg (7-12 lb).
    Colors : White,black,blue,chocolate,red and cream like combinations.
    Lifespan : 12-17 years

    Price : 6,000 to 10,000

  • British Shorthair

    The British Shorthair is a very pleasant cat to have as a companion. It is easy going and placid. It is a loyal, loving cat and will attach itself to the family members.

    Height : For Males: 23-36 cm (9-14 in) , For Females : 20-33 cm (8-13 in).
    Weight: For Males: 4-7 kg (9-17 lb) , For Females : 3-5 kg (6-12 lb).
    Colors : Blue,white,black,red and cream like combinations.
    Lifespan : 12-17 years

    Price : 15,000 to 20,000

  • American

    American is extremely smart and even tempered. It is not very chatty, preferring to chirp or make sweet squeak sounds. It also Behaves gently around children.

    Height : For Males 23-36 cm (10-14 in) , For Females 23-33 cm (9-13 in).
    Weight: For Males: 5-7 kg (11-15 lb) , For Females : 3.5-5.5 kg (8-12 lb).
    Colors : Black,cream,white,blue,red with patterns
    Lifespan : 12-15 years

    Price : 20,000 to 25,000

  • Russian Blue

    Russian Blue cats are known for being well-behaved, gentle and quiet. They will enjoy the company of the humans, but they may be shy and reserved around new people.

    Height : For Males 25-30 cm (10-12 in), For Females 23-28 cm (9-11 in).
    Weight: For Males: 4.5-5.5 kg (10-12 lb) , For Females : 3.5-4.5 kg (8-10 lb).
    Colors : Blueish-gray color, with silvery sheen.
    Lifespan : 15-20 years

    Price : 20,000 to 30,000
